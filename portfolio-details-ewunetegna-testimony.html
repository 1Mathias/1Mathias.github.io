Ewunetegna Testimony

Ewunetegna Testimony is a web application that serves as a factual source of truth to "curious" people as they encounter different doubts and insecurities about Jesus. Also it enables users to get different testimony from the site.

  • JWT Authentication
  • Profile Update
  • Testimony Write
  • Category Filter
